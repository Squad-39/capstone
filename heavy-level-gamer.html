User Interactivity Flow

  1. Amalia logs into her Squad Finder account.
  2. Squad Finder takes Amalia to her profile page.
  3. Amalia goes to the squad list page
  4. Squad Finder displays squad list.
  5. Amalia wants to narrow her search, so she clicks on filter.
  6. Squad Finder filter displays genres; Fighting, Sports, FPS, MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena.
  7. Amalia clicks on FPS from the filter list.
  8. Squad Finder displays all FPS posts from other squads.
  9. Amalia narrows the search down by clicking on Call of Duty from the filter list to see the available squads she can join.
  10. Squad Finder displays all available Call of Duty posts from other squads.
  11. Amalia searches the list and finds a squad that she wants to join.
  12. Amalia clicks on the squad.
  13. Squad Finder displays the squad's profile.
  14. Amalia reviews squad profile
  15. Amalia clicks the join button and awaits for squad leader to accept.
  16. The squad leader accepts Amalia's request to join.
  17. Squad Finder presents Amalia, with an approval message in her notifications.
  18. Amalia clicks on the notification and accepts.
  19. Squad Finder reveals squad message area.
  20. Amalia greets the squad with a message in the squad message area.
  21. Squad Finder posts Amalia's message in the squad mates room.
  22. Squad Finder posts response from other squad members in the squad message area.
  23. Amalia and squad exchange information to meet and play Call of Duty.
