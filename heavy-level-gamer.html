  1. Amalia logs into her Windows profile.
  2. Windows boot all of Amalia's startup programs.
  3. Amalia mouses over her web browser and double clicks to open.
  4. Her web browser opens up to her configured home page.
  5. Amalia goes and types Squad Finder in her search bar of her web browser.
  6. Squad Finder loads to home screen.
  7. Amalia logs into her Squad Finder account.
  8. Squad Finder takes Amalia to her profile page.
  9. Amalia boots Call of Duty.
  10. Call of Duty displays the main menu screen.
  11. Amalia goes back to Squad Finder and searches the initial posts from other Squad Finder members in the main forum.
  12. Amalia then clicks on the filter to see the available filters to choose from.
  13. Squad Finder displays the main categories by genres to list; FPS, MMO (Massive Multiplayer Online), Adventure, Racing, Sports, and Casual.
  14. Amalia clicks on FPS from the filter list.
  15. Squad Finder displays all FPS posts from other Squad Finder members.
  16. Amalia narrows the search down by clicking on Call of Duty from the filter list to see the available squad teams she can join.
  17. Squad Finder displays all available Call of Duty posts from other Squad Finder members.
  18. Amalia searches the list of posts.

  20. Amalia clicks on the squad member's profile.
  21. Squad Finder displays the member's profile to Amalia.
  22. Amalia notices that this squad member has a lot of profile likes.

  25. Amalia clicks the join button to join the chat lobby.

  27. The other members exchange gamertags in the message text area.
