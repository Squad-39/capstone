Loves to play video games and coach e-sports. Card wants to find good talent that practices, doesn't display toxic behavior, and willing to take constructive criticism whenever they need it. Doesn't mind losing or winning as long as everyone puts effort. Will get excited if hidden talent is discovered.

Card is always left frustrated with discord due to never finding anyone new and not matching his particular needs to recruit. Card needs a system where he can find certain gamers with skill levels and experience that he is looking for. Would love to see verified rank and history as to not be fooled.

Goals

  • Find the right gamers

  • Have fun.

User story

Card is an esports headcoach/recruiter looking to build a new team.

Pre condition and Post condition

Pre: Card logs in to Squad finder. Post: Card has recruited gamers and will now begin to practice.

Interactive Flow

  • Card logs in to squad finder.
  • Squad finder loads up home screen.
  • Card logs on his profile to check any notifications or messages.
  • Squad finder loads up cards profile.
  • Card chooses games
  • Squad finder has a dropdown that show individual games.
  • Card goes to rocket league section to check if anyone is looking for a team.
  • Squad finder loads up rocket league users.
  • Card finds a few gamers that match what he is looking for.
  • Card messages those gamers he found that have a lot of potential.
  • Squad finder delivers message to selected gamers.
  • Card makes a post putting in details what he is looking to find for his team.
  • Squad finder populates post onto feed.
  • Card received a few interest messages.
  • Squad finder leaves notifications for Cards profile.
  • Card interacts with the gamers checking on their history and rank.
  • Card sets up a real world try out with the gamers with dates and times.
  • Card goes back to squad finder and closes his post, so he won't receive any more notifications for the recruiting.
  • Squad finder no longer populates the post on the feed, the post is now closed.
  • Card logs out and closes app
